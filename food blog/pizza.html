Homemade Pizza

Easy homemade pizza from scratch

This recipe is for home made pizza

Ingridients

  • pizza dough
  • pizza (or pasta) sauce
  • Your choice of cheese
  • pepporoni

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the overn to 475 degrees
    2. Roll out your pizza dough to the size of the pizza you want
    3. (optional) lightly coat the top of the pizza dough in oil to prevent the dough from getting soggy
    4. Evenly spread the sauce on the dough
    5. Shred the cheese and spread out over the sauce
    6. Add the pepporoni atop the cheese
    7. Place the pizza on a pizza pan and bake for 12-15 minutes
    8. Cut the pizza while hot and serve
