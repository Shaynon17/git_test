Pizza Dough

Easy pizza dough from scratch

This recipe is for pizza dough made at home

Ingridients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 2/3rd cup lukewarm water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoons virgin olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt

    • Steps

    1. Combine the water, instant yeast, and sugar into a bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes
    2. Add the flour, oil, and salt. Mix until the dough is no longer sticky.
    3. knead the dough for 3-4 minutes
    4. Put the dough in a bowl covered by a towel and lit sit for 60 - 90 minutes to let it rise
    5. knead the dough one more time after it has risen
    6. Cut the dough in half. One half can be used for pizza, the other half used or frozen.
    7. Roll out the dough into a circle
