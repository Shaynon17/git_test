Ingridients

  • 12 jalepenos
  • 1 8oz block of pepperjack Cheese
  • 1 8oz package of cream Cheese
  • 24 slices of bacon
  • Tooth picks

    • Steps

    1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
    2. Combine the cream cheese and pepperjack cheese together in a bowl
    3. slice the jalepenos down the center length wise and scoop out the seeds. (leave the seeds in if you want extra spice)
    4. fill the jalepeno halves with the cheese filling
    5. wrap the jalepeno slice with a slice of bacon. Use a toothe pick to hold down the ends of the bacon
    6. Bake on a parchment sheet ontop of a baking sheet for 20 minutes or until the cheese browns
    7. Let cool for 2 minutes, then serve
